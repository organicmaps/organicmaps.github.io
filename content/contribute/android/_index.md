### **About Organic Maps**

Organic Maps is a community-driven navigation app focused on privacy. Designed for
travelers, drivers, hikers, and cyclists, it leverages crowd-sourced data from
OpenStreetMap with contributions from users globally. The app prioritizes privacy,
with no location tracking, no data collection, and no communication with third-party servers.
All Organic Maps features work offline, offering reliable navigation even in remote
areas with no cellular signal. As an open-source project, Organic Maps emphasizes
community development and collaboration.

### Vision

Our vision is to provide free and open navigation with built-in privacy, developed
by the community. Organic Maps aims to build a navigation app that serves the public
good, prioritizing the needs of users over the interests of big tech, VCs, or banks.
